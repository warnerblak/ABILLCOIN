A Bill to a Bill

1. Introduction

$ABILL is a community-driven meme coin with a mission: take a bill (a humble proposal) and turn it into a bill (a billion-dollar market cap). The essence of $ABILL lies in its double meaning — it represents the fun, irreverent side of meme coins, but also carries a purpose-driven path to community wealth creation.

$ABILL is officially launched on Pump.Fun, a decentralized platform that simplifies token creation and trading, enabling easy access for new holders and providing the foundation for organic community growth.

2. Vision

$ABILL represents both financial freedom and collective power. Just like a bill that gets passed through a vote, $ABILL’s destiny lies in the hands of the community. Our vision is to build a strong, decentralized community with shared goals, token utility that rewards holders, and a culture of holding until we reach the billion-dollar milestone.
